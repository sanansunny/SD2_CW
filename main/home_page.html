About us

Nowadays, especially in London we have many people looking for tickets for nightclubs and festivals but most of the population is facing a common issue that tickets go fast. We created this platform to give you the oportunity to find tickets even though the event might be sold-out.

People buy tickets and change their minds about attending all the time. People also try to purchase tickets too late at times. Here at mumble mumble, we saw an opportunity to solve two problems at once.

This app will give you the oportunity to find tickets when a person is resaling their tickets in the platform so people can buy tickets still even though the event might be sold-out.
